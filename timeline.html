Timeline: A Decade of Deception

Explore the chronology of alleged systematic misconduct, revealing patterns of faulty vehicles, VIN tampering, and document falsification spanning over a decade.

Key Events and Patterns:

Below is a public-safe chronology outlining significant events and identified patterns of alleged misconduct. This timeline is continuously updated as new information and evidence come to light.

  • 2015

    First Traceable Incidents of Negligence

    Early reports and internal complaints regarding insufficient vehicle maintenance and safety concerns begin to surface from drivers and mechanics employed by [Defendants].

  • 2017

    Emergence of VIN Tampering Practices

    Evidence suggests the beginning of systematic VIN tampering on written-off or heavily damaged vehicles, allowing them to bypass regulatory checks and re-enter circulation.

  • 2019

    Pattern of Blame-Shifting and Document Backdating Intensifies

    Following a series of mechanical failures and incidents, a clear pattern emerges where drivers are unjustly blamed, and critical maintenance or incident reports are allegedly backdated or altered.

  • 2023

    Increased Financial Hardship and Threats to Employees

    More instances of drivers facing wage deductions, legal threats, or being pushed out of work after vehicle incidents linked to mechanical failures or VIN fraud.

  • 2025

    Ongoing Operation of "Rebirthed" Vehicles

    As of 2025, vehicles known to have undergone suspicious VIN changes or to have histories of being written off are still found to be actively operating, posing ongoing safety risks.

  • Present

    Fair Go Justice Movement Initiates Class Action Investigation

    Based on mounting evidence and victim testimonies, the Fair Go Justice Movement launches a formal investigation into a potential class action against [Defendants].
