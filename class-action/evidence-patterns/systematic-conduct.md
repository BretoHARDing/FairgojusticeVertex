*Dates showing repeated conduct over time*

*Evidence of others affected by same scheme*

- **Document Templates:** Look for standardized, backdated document templates used to shift blame after incidents.
- **HR Records:** Examine disciplinary records for patterns of blaming drivers for mechanical failures.
